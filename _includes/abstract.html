Abstract submission

Abstract submission is now open and final date for the submission is June 16 2019.

Prior to submission, please note that abstracts for invited talks and contributed posters are to be submitted as a one-page PDF file through the online submission system on this website. Abstracts should follow the LaTeX template or the Word template of ISTCP-2019. A brief synopsis of up to 500 characters is also required for online submission of the abstract. We strongly recommend to prepare the synopsis in advance and copy it into the submission form. The final conference website will contain the title, the name of the presenting author, the short synopsis, and link to the full abstract as PDF.
