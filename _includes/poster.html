Posters

There will be two poster sessions during the conference, and participants are encouraged to submit abstracts for posters, following the general guidelines for conference abstracts. Poster boards have a height of 2.40 meters and a width of 1.0 meter, and will fit regular A0 posters in portrait mode.

Abstract submission will open on January 1 2019, and the deadline for submitting poster abstracts is June 16 2019.
