About ISTCP

Held every three years, the congresses of the International Society of Theoretical Chemical Physics (ISTCP) continue the tradition of showcasing the achievement and advances of all areas of theoretical chemical physics, with special emphasis on the interaction between experimental and theoretical physical chemistry, chemical physics, materials and life sciences.
