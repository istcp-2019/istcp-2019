Climate, weather and clothing

Tromsø enjoys a tempered climate due to the North Atlantic Current that extends the Gulf stream to Northern Europe. The average temperatures thus ranges from from -4.4 °C in the coldest month of February to 11.8 °C in the warmest month of July.

In July, temperatures can be expected to vary from 5°C to above 20°C, and may include rain showers, though we in general do not get very heavy rain showers. Warm clothes are thus recommended for most travelers, but also summer clothing should the weather be favorable.
