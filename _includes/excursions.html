Conference Excursions

The conference organizers together with Moment Norway provide you with various opportunities for excursions during the conference, in particular for the free afternoon on July 14. During registration, you will be able to sign up to these excursions. If you sign up for an excursion, this is a binding registration. You will however not pay the excursion now, payment will be made directly to Moment Norway during the conference. For more information regarding the excursions, please contact Moment Norway directly.
