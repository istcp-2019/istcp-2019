Venue

The conference will be held at Clarion Hotel The Edge , which is also the recommended hotel for accommodation.

The hotel was opened in 2014, and has won a number of national and international hotel awards , and is ranked #9 in the 2017 TripAdvisor Travellers Award .

The hotel is situated at the waterfront in downtown Tromsø, providing excellent views of the surrounding mountains and fjords, while being located at the centre of the lively downtown area.

We have negotiated very favorable accommodation prices in the hotel and reserved all rooms in the hotel for the conference, which we believe will help stimulate interactions and create a sense of intimacy while the scope of the talks and posters will give a comprehensive overview of the field of theoretical chemical physics.

As the sun will not set during the conference, we expect the Skybar on the 11th floor to be a favorite location for scientific discussion long into the night, but a variety of places are of course available in downtown Tromsø.
