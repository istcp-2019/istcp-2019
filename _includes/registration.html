Registration

Registration is now open. Early-bird registration will close on April 30 2019, and final date for registration will be June 16 2019.

Registration fees:

  • Regular participants: 3000 NOK (4000 NOK after April 30)
  • PhD/Master students: 2250 NOK (letter of confirmation from supervisor required)
  • Keynote/plenary speakers: Free
  • Invited speakers/session chairs/committee members: 2000 NOK
  • Accompanying persons: 2000 NOK

The registration includes access to all scientific sessions and the welcome reception. Light food and a limited number of drinks are included in the registration fee for the welcome reception and the two poster sessions. You may during registration sign up for a rich lunch buffet on one or more days of the conference (295 NOK/day) as well as for the conference dinner (750 NOK/person).

During registration, the conference organizers together with Moment Norway provide you also with the opportunity to sign up for a conference excursion, in particular for the free afternoon on July 14. Note that signing up for an excursion means a binding registration. You will however not pay the excursion now, payment will be made directly to Moment Norway during the conference.

After registration, you will be provided with a hotel registration code that you can use to book hotel rooms at a discounted rate of 900 NOK/night at Clarion Hotel The Edge (conference venue) or Clarion Collection Hotel With, both providing a full breakfast buffet included in the price, or at Comfort Hotel Xpress Tromsø for 750 NOK/night. Rooms are available on a first come, first served basis. Discounted prices will be available until April 30 2019.

The discounted rates are available for the period 9-19 July, allowing you to use the opportunity to stay in Tromsø also beyond the conference. Check out also our page for possible vacation offers in or near Tromsø.
